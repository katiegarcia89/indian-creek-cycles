About Indian Creek Cycles

Our Story

Indian Creek Cycles was created to make it easier for riders to browse bikes, plan routes, reserve with confidence, and get out on the trail with less friction.

This website was built as a collaborative team project focused on creating a polished bike-rental experience from first click to confirmed reservation.

From reservations and waivers to reviews, profiles, admin tools, and ride-planning support, every part of the site was shaped to feel welcoming, practical, and ready for real use.

Our Mission & Values

Passion for Cycling

We believe cycling is more than transportation—it's a way to connect with nature, stay healthy, and create lasting memories with loved ones.

Quality & Safety

Every bike in our fleet is meticulously maintained and safety-checked before each rental. Your safety is our top priority.

Family Friendly

We welcome riders of all ages and skill levels. From training wheels to high-end mountain bikes, we have something for everyone.

Environmental Stewardship

We're committed to preserving the natural beauty of our trails. We practice and promote responsible, eco-friendly cycling.
