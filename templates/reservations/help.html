03. Unlock & Return

📍

Go to your dock

Find your bike at your selected Smart Dock Location.

🔓

Unlock your bike

  • Option 1: Show your QR code from your confirmation email to the dock scanner to unlock your bike.
  • Alternatively: Scan the QR code with your phone to open your ride dashboard for unlocking, checking dock availability, or managing your reservation.
    • No internet? The dock can unlock your bike directly using your QR code.
↩️

Return your bike

  • Return your bike to any ICC Smart Dock location or the ICC Hub.
  • Bikes must be returned to a dock — they cannot be left on the trail.

Hardware Tip: Look for flashing green lights on the dock!

Tip: Check your dashboard before returning to see which docks have open space.
